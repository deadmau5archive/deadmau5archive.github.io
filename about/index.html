> About Website Text Goes Here <

On this page, you can find reuploads of deadmau5 livestreams (from twitch, mixer and mau5trap.tv).
The videos are hosted on YouTube (unlisted however). You can browse them here on this page.

The point of this whole thing is to back up the VODs of deadmau5.
Mixer, where Joel was streaming after leaving twitch, deleted VODs after about 14 days, and now it's completely shut down.
mau5trap.tv, Joel's own streaming platform he had for a while, didn't even have VODs, but I managed to record the streams anyway.
Before mixer and mau5trap.tv, Joel was streaming on twitch. Currently, older VODs on twitch are still online, but should they ever decide to delete them, they're backed up here as well.
Currently, Joel is back on Twitch, but newer streams aren't saved at all, because Joel has currently turned off the VOD feature.

Link conversion guide


This page is designed to easily be able to convert URLs of stream VODs or videos into a link to the respective video page on the archive, if it exists.
To convert a link you have, simply replace the domain name with deadmau5archive.github.io.

Examples:
Twitch: https://www.twitch.tv/videos/378090826 → https://deadmau5archive.github.io/videos/378090826
Mixer: https://mixer.com/deadmau5?vod=5KxZ7vVfw02kMD6WxqdndQ → https://deadmau5archive.github.io/deadmau5?vod=5KxZ7vVfw02kMD6WxqdndQ
YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Z1t8e2jpuOI → https://deadmau5archive.github.io/watch?v=Z1t8e2jpuOI
youtu.be: https://youtu.be/Z1t8e2jpuOI → https://deadmau5archive.github.io/Z1t8e2jpuOI

Some links may redirect to another page within the archive.
deadmau5 never streamed on YouTube but what I mean by converting YouTube links is that when you have a YouTube link to a video from the archive channel, you can use this to get to the respective archive video page.


Usage etiquette


Rips disclaimer
Please do not create rips from these streams and upload WIPs to your own youtube channel (or even monetize them )
Joel has repeatedly asked the community to not do that (see here) and I urge you to please respect this.
Also, it's really not worth it anyway because mau5trap does actively scan YouTube for stream rips and takes them down, and the same will happen to you.

REMEMBER
Joel says:
IF YOU CREATE RIPS I WILL RIP OFF YOUR HEAD


Crediting
If you create stream clips/moments etc. on your channel taken from the archive, feel free! I love deadmau5 clips.
However, I am asking you to link to the original video you got it from please.
this is not because I care about exposure (I dont give a shit, all videos are unlisted anyway), it's just about not having the frustration of missing context if you want to watch more of a clip or want to know on what date something happened on stream or whatever.
(basically just like there's a "watch full vod" link on twitch for clips)
DECLARE YOUR SOURCES PEOPLE IT'S NOT THAT HARD!
thank you (:
