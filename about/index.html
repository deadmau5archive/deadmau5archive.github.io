On this page, you can find reuploads of deadmau5 twitch streams.
The videos are hosted on YouTube, but you can browse them more easily on this page. Also the design of this page is heavily inspired by twitch.
The point of this whole thing is to back up the VODs of deadmau5, should twitch ever decide to delete them.
Other than that, YouTube's player is a lot faster and more responsive than Twitch's player, so that's a plus I guess.

Some fun facts:
There are currently 874 videos here on this page.
This is a total of 119.977 days of video material, and about 7.201 TB big in the highest resolution.
