On this page, you can find reuploads of deadmau5 livestreams (from twitch and mixer).
The videos are hosted on YouTube, but you can browse them more easily on this page. Also the design of this page is sorta inspired by twitch.

The point of this whole thing is to back up the VODs of deadmau5.
Mixer, where Joel is streaming now, deletes VODs after about 14 days. Before mixer, Joel was streaming on twitch. As of time of writing this, the VODs on twitch are still online, but should they ever decide to delete them, they're backed up here.
Other than that, YouTube's player is a lot faster and more responsive than Twitch's player, so that's a plus I guess.

Some fun facts:
There are currently 41 videos from mixer and 874 videos from twitch here on this page (915 total).
This is a total of 127.437 days of video material!
All the twitch videos collectively are about 7.201 TB big in the highest resolution.
