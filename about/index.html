On this page, you can find reuploads of deadmau5 livestreams (from twitch and mixer).
The videos are hosted on YouTube, but you can browse them more easily on this page.

The point of this whole thing is to back up the VODs of deadmau5.
Mixer, where Joel was streaming after leaving twitch, deleted VODs after about 14 days, and now it's completely shut down.
Before mixer, Joel was streaming on twitch. Currently, the VODs on twitch are still online, but should they ever decide to delete them, they're backed up here as well.


Some fun facts:
There are currently 212 videos from mixer and 874 videos from twitch here on this page (1086 total).
This is a total of 162.724 days of video material!
All the twitch videos collectively are about 7.201 TB big in the highest resolution. And about 1.838 TB for mixer.
